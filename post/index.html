First thoughts about using Hugo

Hugo, a fast and modern static site engine. When I first heard of it, it seems like a good idea. It still is! But after using it you quickly realise its not as perfect as it seems. You quickly realise that Hugo is built for the more tech savy users and lack any form of guidance for those less tech savvy. No doubt they have a great documentation, but users nowadays are too impatient to flip through pages of documentation to get their first site up.
